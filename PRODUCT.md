# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

SUN.STUDIO slide creators, reviewers, mentors, and internal teams who need polished training, onboarding, workshop, and presentation materials from structured prompts and approved brand resources.

## Product Purpose

This workspace turns source prompts, documents, brand packs, and published visual-library items into reviewable, packaged slide decks and reusable visual artifacts. Success means outputs are content-faithful, on brand, editable where required, and shipped with clear source authority, QA evidence, checksums, and delivery manifests.

## Brand Personality

Friendly, skilled, reliable. The voice should feel energetic but professional, coaching rather than lecturing, and concise enough for internal presentations.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI slide templates, stock-looking visuals, overused glass cards, decorative gradients without content purpose, unsupported staging components, and changes to historical phase outputs. Do not move or duplicate canonical SUN.STUDIO assets.

## Design Principles

- Preserve source authority before visual invention.
- Use one strong visual anchor per slide.
- Prefer published reusable resources before creating slide-local structures.
- Keep foreground content editable and reviewable.
- Package every delivery with limitations, checksums, and QA evidence.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target clear projection readability at 1920x1080, strong text contrast, restrained motion with reduced-motion support, no overlapping text, and slide layouts that remain legible when scaled in the deck viewer.
